Fanyu Wang

Fanyu Wang received the Master of Engineering degree from Jiangnan University, Wuxi, China. He serves as a member of ColeGroup, Key Laboratory of Media Design and Software Technology in Jiangsu Province, advised by Prof. Zhenping Xie. His research interests include the interpretable deep learning methods in Natural Language Processing, which include Chinese spelling correction and Chinese grammatical correction. Download my CV
