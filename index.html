Fanyu Wang

Fanyu Wang is currently pursuing his PhD in HumanISE Lab<\a> at Monash University, under the guidance of Dr. Chetan Arora<\a>. He holds a Master of Engineering degree from Jiangnan University, Wuxi, China, and is a member of the ColeGroup at the Key Laboratory of Media Design and Software Technology in Jiangsu Province, where he is advised by Prof. Zhenping Xie. Fanyu's research interests previously focus on LLMs driven Requirement Engineering, and interpretable deep learning methods in Natural Language Processing. Download my CV
